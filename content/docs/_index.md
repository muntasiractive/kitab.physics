# Introduction

Welcome to **Kitab.Physics**, an open-source documentation database dedicated to Physics.

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive, community-driven resource for learners and researchers of all levels, covering everything from classical mechanics to quantum physics and relativity.

## Our Goal

To create a centralized, searchable, and collaborative knowledge base that empowers students and enthusiasts to understand the laws that govern our universe.
